Rocket Systems Overview

From structural design to flight validation

Structural Design

Airframe and internal structure designed using CAD and finite element analysis to ensure stiffness, mass efficiency, and survivability under launch loads.

Trajectory Analysis

Numerical simulation of ascent trajectory considering thrust, drag, mass variation, and atmospheric conditions.

Propulsion

Solid rocket motor characterization through thrust curve measurement, chamber pressure analysis, and performance validation.

Velocity Profile

Velocity and acceleration reconstruction from onboard sensor data to validate flight performance.

Recovery System

Dual-deployment recovery architecture with drogue and main parachutes for controlled descent and safe payload recovery.

Avionics

Custom onboard computer handling telemetry, data logging, sensor fusion, and flight event detection.

Guidance & Stability

Passive stabilization through fin design and center of pressure optimization to ensure stable ascent.

Testing & Validation

Ground testing, static fire campaigns, and flight tests to iteratively validate subsystem performance.
